Real Estate Market Trends & Local Regulations (2026)

## Market Trends
- High Demand for Suburban Properties: Since remote work continues to be popular, spacious homes with extra bedrooms and yards (parking, guestroom) have seen a 15% increase in value over the last two years.
- Premium on Air Conditioning: Due to rising summer temperatures, homes with central air conditioning command up to an 8-10% premium compared to similar homes without it.
- Basement value: A finished basement significantly boosts property appeal, specifically for growing families. It can add up to 5-10% to the final valuation in prime neighborhoods.
- Mainroad Access: Properties directly on main roads tend to have a dual valuation base. While they are slightly less desired for quiet residential living (down 2%), they hold 10-15% higher value if commercial zoning is permitted.

## Local Regulations
- Structural Expansion Limits: Any home expanding beyond 3 stories must obtain a special municipal permit. 
- Environmental Upgrades: Properties without energy-efficient hot water heating systems may soon face local compliance taxes.
- Parking Requirements: New zoning laws require at least 1 designated parking spot per property with 3 or more bedrooms. Properties failing to meet this standard may incur slight penalties on market value.
